    About Me

    Hi, I'm Nathaniel Moreno

    I am an aspiring IT professional with a strong passion for cybersecurity, network management, and systems administration. As a junior cybersecurity analyst, I am responsible for monitoring network traffic, identifying potential threats, and implementing preventive measures to safeguard organizational data.

    I also work closely with the cyber ops associate team to analyze security incidents, perform vulnerability assessments, and maintain system integrity across multiple platforms. My experience in operating systems support has strengthened my ability to troubleshoot complex technical issues, manage software updates, and ensure the reliability of both Windows and Linux environments. In addition, I collaborate with network technicians to maintain stable and secure network connections, configure routers and switches, and assist in the implementation of network security protocols.

    I am highly committed to continuous learning and professional growth, staying updated with the latest developments in cybersecurity and IT infrastructure. My goal is to contribute to a forward-thinking organization where I can apply my technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and attention to detail to protect digital assets and enhance overall system performance.
